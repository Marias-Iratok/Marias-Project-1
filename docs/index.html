Gannavaram



Vision:

To establish an excellent, value-based Education-Hub to meet the Challenges of Global Competitiveness for multi-dimensional growth of the institution and effective industry-institute interface, contributing in turn towards the growth of a healthy and happy Society.


Mission:

Govt.Polytechnic, Gannavaram aims at creating excellent workforce to serve industry through various activities planned in consultation with industry. The institution focuses on well planned process for overall grooming of the student’s personality particularly on attitude, teamwork, hard work, character building which can be achieved by cultural, sports, departmental student associations, projects, Industry-Academia partnership, etc
